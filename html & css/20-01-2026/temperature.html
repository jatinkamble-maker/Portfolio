Temperature and Weight Converstion

Celsius to Fahrenheit Converstion

The formula to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit is:

F = (C x 9/5) + 32

Fahrenheit to Celsius Converstion

The formula to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius is:

C = (F - 32) x 5/9

Kilograms to Pounds Converstion

The formula to Kilograms to Pounds is:

lbs = kg x 2.20462

Pounds to Kilograms Converstion

The formula to convert Pounds to Kilograms is:

kg = lbs ÷ 2.20462
